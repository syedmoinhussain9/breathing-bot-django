Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Note: Breathing Bot is currently in an experimental beta phase. Features may be updated, and your feedback is highly appreciated as we improve the experience.
What we collect

When you create an account, we store:

  • Username and email address
  • Hashed password (we never store your plain password)
  • Breathing and meditation session history (technique, cycles, date)
  • Language preference
  • Profile picture URL (a link you provide — we don't store image files)
What we don't do
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not share your session history or health-related data with advertisers.
  • We do not collect health or medical data.
Advertising

Breathing Bot displays ads to support free access to the app. Our ad partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

  • We use Google AdSense (or similar) to serve ads.
  • Ad partners may collect device identifiers, IP address, and browsing behaviour.
  • You can opt out of personalised ads via your Google account settings or your device's ad settings.
  • Logged-in users' breathing session data is never shared with advertisers.
Guest users

If you use Breathing Bot without creating an account, we store one functional cookie — your language preference — so the app remembers your chosen language between visits. No personal data, no session history, and no tracking by us beyond what ad partners may collect.

Your rights

You can:

  • Export your session history from your profile page at any time.
  • Delete your account by contacting us — all your data will be permanently removed.
  • Use the app as a guest with no account and no data stored by us.
Data storage

Your data is stored on a secure server. We use Django's built-in security features including password hashing, CSRF protection, and session management.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at breathing_bot_syed@outlook.com
